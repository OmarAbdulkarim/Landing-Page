03 — About

Behind the work

I work at the intersection of engineering and analysis — the place where a plan on paper meets the data that tells you whether it's working.

Most of what I build starts the same way: a repetitive task, a spreadsheet stretched past its limits, or a question nobody could answer quickly. I turn those into tools people actually use — automations, dashboards, and models that save hours and remove guesswork.

Whether I'm planning a project, analyzing a dataset, or advising a team, the approach is the same: understand the problem deeply, keep the solution simple, and make sure it holds up in practice.
