SEC. 03 — Approach

Behind the work

"Every tool here started as a bottleneck — a spreadsheet past its limits, a question nobody could answer fast."

This practice sits at the intersection of engineering and analysis — the place where a plan on paper meets the data that says whether it's working.

The pattern is always the same: a repetitive task, a manual process stretched too far, a decision made on gut feel because the numbers took too long. Those become tools people actually use — automations, dashboards, and models that remove hours and guesswork.

The method doesn't change with the job: understand the problem deeply, keep the solution simple, and make sure it holds up in the field.
